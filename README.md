## 💡 Tips for Sustainable Commuting in Boulder

1. **Use the Boulder Creek Path** — The path along Boulder Creek provides scenic and safe routes for both walkers and cyclists.
2. **Check the Weather** — Boulder's microclimate can change quickly. Dress in layers and carry rain gear.
3. **Explore Bike-Share** — Boulder B-cycle offers bike-sharing for those who don't want to maintain a personal bike.
4. **Combine Modes** — Use a combination of walking and biking with public transit (RTD buses) for longer commutes.
5. **Carpool When Necessary** — For days when walking/biking isn't feasible, carpooling reduces per-person emissions significantly.

## 🌎 Environmental Impact

By choosing to walk or bike instead of driving:
- **Per trip**: Avoid ~1.6 kg of CO₂ emissions
- **Per year (5 days/week)**: Avoid ~400 kg of CO₂ emissions
- **Per year**: Get ~100+ hours of physical exercise
- **Community**: Reduce traffic congestion and parking demand
