## 🚶‍♂️ Walking Route

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Distance** | 4.03 km (2.5 miles) |
| **Estimated Time** | ~48–60 minutes |
| **Route** | Oak Avenue → 19th Street → 20th St. → 20th Street → Pearl Street → 18th Street → Canyon Boulevard |

### Turn-by-Turn Walking Directions:

1. **Start on Oak Avenue** — Head south on Oak Avenue (97 m)
2. **Turn onto 19th Street** — Continue south on 19th Street (2.16 km) — *the longest segment*
3. **Connect to 20th St.** — Brief connector (106 m)
4. **Continue on 20th Street** — Head south (566 m)
5. **Turn onto Pearl Street** — Cross onto Pearl Street (216 m)
6. **Continue on 18th Street** — Head south (249 m)
7. **Final stretch on Canyon Boulevard** — Continue south on Canyon Boulevard to your destination (637 m)

### Walking Tips:
- 🌄 The route involves a consistent southward descent, making the return trip uphill
- ☀️ Boulder's high altitude (5,430 ft) means the air is thinner — pace yourself!
- 🚦 Plan for traffic light waits at major intersections on Pearl Street and Canyon Boulevard
- 🌺 Ideal in spring/summer/fall; bring layers as Boulder weather can change quickly

Boulder is one of the most bike-friendly cities in the US, with:
- 🛣️ Dedicated bike paths along Canyon Boulevard and Pearl Street
- 🌄 Scenic routes through the foothills
- 🏔️ Year-round cycling weather for much of the year
- 🚲 Extensive bike lane infrastructure
